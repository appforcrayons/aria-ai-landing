Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 24, 2026  |  Effective Date: February 24, 2026

Aria AI Answering Service ("Aria," "we," "us," or "our") is operated by Appetite for Crayons. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and protect information from visitors to our website and customers of our AI answering service. By using our website or services, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information in the following ways:

  • Information you provide directly: Name, phone number, email address, business name, and zip code when you submit a demo request or booking form.
  • Call and interaction data: When Aria answers calls on behalf of our HVAC clients, we may record call transcripts and appointment data to facilitate service delivery.
  • Website usage data: Pages visited, time on site, browser type, device type, and IP address via standard web analytics.
  • Cookies and tracking technologies: We use cookies to improve website performance and analyze visitor behavior. You may disable cookies in your browser settings.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Schedule and confirm HVAC service appointments
  • Send SMS appointment confirmations, reminders, and follow-up messages (only when you have provided explicit consent)
  • Respond to demo requests and sales inquiries
  • Improve our AI answering service
  • Send service-related communications
  • Comply with legal obligations

3. SMS Messaging & Opt-In

We will only send you SMS messages if you have explicitly opted in by checking the consent box on our scheduling form. We do not send unsolicited text messages.

By opting in to SMS communications, you consent to receive the following types of messages from Aria AI Answering Service:

  • Appointment confirmation messages
  • Appointment reminder messages (sent 24 hours and/or 1 hour before your appointment)
  • Service follow-up messages
  • Demo scheduling confirmations and reminders

Message frequency: Message frequency varies based on your appointment schedule. Most customers receive 2–4 messages per appointment cycle.

Message and data rates may apply. Standard message and data rates charged by your wireless carrier may apply to messages you receive from us.

How to opt out: You may opt out of SMS messages at any time by replying STOP to any message we send. After you send STOP, you will receive one final confirmation message and no further messages will be sent. To re-subscribe, reply START.

For help: Reply HELP to any message for assistance, or contact us directly at app4crayons@gmail.com.

4. Mobile Information Sharing Statement

Mobile opt-in information and consent data are never shared with third parties for marketing or promotional purposes. We do not sell, rent, or lease your mobile phone number to any third party.

Your mobile number may be shared with our SMS platform provider (GoHighLevel) solely for the purpose of delivering the messages you consented to receive. This service provider is contractually prohibited from using your information for any other purpose.

5. Cookie & Tracking Practices

Our website uses the following types of cookies:

  • Essential cookies: Required for basic website functionality.
  • Analytics cookies: Help us understand how visitors interact with our site (e.g., Google Analytics). These are anonymized and do not personally identify you.
  • Marketing cookies: Used to track conversions from advertising campaigns. You may opt out via your browser settings or Google's ad settings.

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect some website functionality.

6. Data Security and Handling

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. These measures include:

  • HTTPS encryption for all data transmitted through our website
  • Access controls limiting who can view customer data
  • Secure third-party platforms (GoHighLevel, Stripe) that maintain their own security certifications

No method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Third-Party Service Providers

We use the following third-party services to operate our business:

  • GoHighLevel: CRM, SMS messaging, and appointment scheduling platform
  • Stripe: Payment processing (we do not store your full payment card information)
  • Calendly: Demo call scheduling
  • Google Analytics: Website analytics

These providers have their own privacy policies governing their use of data.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal information we hold about you
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal data (subject to legal retention requirements)
  • Opt out of SMS: Reply STOP to any message at any time
  • Opt out of marketing: Contact us to be removed from any marketing communications

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at app4crayons@gmail.com.

9. Data Retention

We retain personal information for as long as necessary to provide our services and comply with legal obligations. Appointment records are typically retained for 2 years. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.

10. Children's Privacy

Our services are intended for business owners and are not directed to individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors. If you believe a minor has provided us information, contact us immediately.
